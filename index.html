Capstone Project

Field Development Plan Gas Well, North Sumatra Basin

This FDP covers gas field development in the North Sumatra Basin derived from geological, geophysical, and reservoir studies to economic feasibility. GGR is estimated through integrated subsurface interpretation, volumetric analysis, and reservoir modeling, followed by recoverable reserves and production forecasting. The development concept defines well planning, drilling and completion strategy, and surface facilities for gas handling and delivery. Economic evaluation combines CAPEX, OPEX, gas price, and fiscal terms to assess viability using NPV, IRR, and breakeven gas price.
